FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is AtusPay safe to use?
Yes. AtusPay operates on licensed wallet-as-a-service rails, with TLS 1.3 encryption in transit and AES-256 at rest. Every login is protected by biometric or PIN auth, and you can freeze any card from the app the moment something looks off.
How long does account opening take?
Most users are spending within 3 minutes. Sign up with your email and phone, enter your BVN to complete KYC, and your dedicated NUBAN account is provisioned automatically.
What does it cost?
Opening an account is free. Virtual cards are free to create. Bank transfers within Nigeria use the standard NIBSS fee. Currency conversion uses a transparent spread shown to you before you confirm.
Which currencies can I hold?
NGN is the default for every account. Additional currencies (USD, GBP, EUR) can be added on demand, with conversions priced live in the app.
Where do you operate?
AtusPay is currently live in Nigeria, with expansion across West Africa planned. Push notifications, multi-language support, and 24/7 in-app support are already shipped.
How do I get support?
Open a ticket from inside the app — every conversation is tied to your account so the support team has context from the first message. Average first response is under an hour.
